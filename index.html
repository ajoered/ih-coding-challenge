WHY ARE WE DOING THIS?

Because coding is the most important job skill of the future, and everybody should get the chance to learn how to code.
As technology takes over the world, having a basic knowledge of coding will become as important as knowing how to read or write. And yet it remains quite inaccessible and a mystery to most. We are launching the Berlin Coding Challenge to show that, with the right guidance, it's something anybody can do.
We have put together a free program that will get you from zero to coding in 2 months. Designed for complete beginners, it's led and guided by experts from the Ironhack team, and meant to be taken at your own pace.

TUTORED

Learning to code on your own can be tough. The challenge is led and supervised by the Ironhack team. We will provide feedback and assistance every step of the way.

FLEXIBLE

Learn at your own pace - you decide how much time you spend. The course is designed for complete beginners, but will challenge the most experienced coders by the end.

SOCIAL

Learning to code is much better with other people. You will be able to meet other participants through our Slack channel, and we will be hosting 3 awesome events in Berlin.
